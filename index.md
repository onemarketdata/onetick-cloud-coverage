# Overview

`OneTick` is a Market Data Analytics platform that can be deployed or provided as a services.
Databases can cover both real time, intra-day and historical access, and can be queried through SQL, along with Python and REST.

This documentation details the market data coverage of OneTick Cloud, the on-demand market data service from OneTick.

Institutions can register for a 30 day free trial at:   `http://www.onetick.com/cloud-services`
