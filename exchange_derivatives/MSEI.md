The Metropolitan Stock Exchange of India (MSEI) offers trading in equities, bonds, ETFs, and derivatives.

* Country: India [IN]
* Mkt Hours: 08:00-17:00
* Time Zone: Asia/Kolkata
* Start Date: 20140129
* Real Time Available: No
* Bars Available: Yes
* Book Depth Available: No
