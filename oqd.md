# OQD - One Quant Data

Reference Data to populate a Security Master, including Daily Price History, Corporate Actions history, Market Calendar and Symbology mapping

It covers:


* `OQD_CACT` - Global Corporate Actions History


* `OQD_DPRC` - Daily Price History for Global Equities


* `OQD_ETF` - US ETF Constituents


* `OQD_FUT` - Daily Price History for Global Futures


* `OQD_MKTCAL` - Historic Market Holiday Calendar and Trading Hours


* `OQD_SEC` - Instrument Security Master


* `OQD_XOID` - Instrument Symbology Cross Reference from OID


* `OQD_XSYM` - Instrument Symbology Cross Reference to OID
