# Vienna Stock Exchange - Indices Trading Hours

The Vienna Stock Exchange provides indices tracking the performance of Austrian and Central and Eastern European equities, including the ATX and CECE indices.

Trading Hours are divided into multiple sessions

#### Vienna Stock Exchange - Indices - Trading Hours

| Session Name   | Time Zone     | Start Time   | End Time   |
|----------------|---------------|--------------|------------|
| PRE_MARKET     | Europe/Vienna | 08:00        | 09:00      |
| MARKET         | Europe/Vienna | 09:00        | 17:34      |
| POST_MARKET    | Europe/Vienna | 17:34        | 17:45      |
