# Budapest Stock Exchange - Indices Trading Hours

The Budapest Stock Exchange trades major Hungarian equity indices like the BUX and BUMIX.

Trading Hours are divided into multiple sessions

#### Budapest Stock Exchange - Indices - Trading Hours

| Session Name   | Time Zone       | Start Time   | End Time   |
|----------------|-----------------|--------------|------------|
| PRE_MARKET     | Europe/Budapest | 08:15        | 09:00      |
| MARKET         | Europe/Budapest | 09:00        | 17:07      |
| POST_MARKET    | Europe/Budapest | 17:07        | 17:20      |
