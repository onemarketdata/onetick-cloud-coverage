# Sample Data

Sample Data is available for all subscribers and those institutions undertaking a 30 day trial.
It provides access to historic data from 1st January 2024 to 31st March 2024.

It covers:

* `AU_COMP_SAMPLE` - Australian Consolidated Equities Sample
* `CA_COMP_SAMPLE` - Canadian Consolidated Market Sample
* `CME_SAMPLE` - CME Group (PCAP) Sample
* `EU_COMP_SAMPLE` - European Consolidated Equities Sample
* `EUREX_SAMPLE` - Eurex Sample
* `GLOBAL_FX_SAMPLE` - Global FX (Spots, Forwards, NDFs) Sample
* `ICE_EU_COM_SAMPLE` - ICE Europe Commodities Sample
* `ICE_US_SAMPLE` - ICE US Sample
* `JP_COMP_SAMPLE` - Japan Consolidated Equities Sample
* `LSE_SAMPLE` - London Stock Exchange - UK Market Sample
* `TDI_FUT_SAMPLE` - Global Curated Futures Sample
* `US_COMP_SAMPLE` - US Consolidated Equities (exc. OTC) Sample
* `US_COMP_REPLAY` - US Consolidated Equity Replay for Intraday / CEP
* `US_OPTIONS_SAMPLE` - US Consolidated Options (OPRA) Sample
* `US_OPTIONS_EOD_SAMPLE` - US Consolidated Options (OPRA) EOD - OPRA Daily Volume, OI and Greeks Sample
