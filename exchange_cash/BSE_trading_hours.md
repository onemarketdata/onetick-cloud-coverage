# BSE (Bombay Stock Exchange) Trading Hours

The Bombay Stock Exchange is one of the largest securities markets. It is located on Dalal Street, Mumbai and lists over 6000 companies.

Trading Hours are divided into multiple sessions

#### BSE (Bombay Stock Exchange) - Trading Hours

| Session Name   | Time Zone     | Start Time   | End Time   |
|----------------|---------------|--------------|------------|
| PRE_MARKET     | Asia/Calcutta | 09:00        | 09:15      |
| MARKET         | Asia/Calcutta | 09:15        | 15:30      |
| POST_MARKET    | Asia/Calcutta | 15:30        | 16:01      |
