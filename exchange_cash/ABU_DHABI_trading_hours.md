# Abu Dhabi Securities Exchange Trading Hours

The Abu Dhabi Securities Exchange (ADX) facilitates trading in equities, bonds, ETFs, and other securities issued by UAE companies.

Trading Hours are divided into multiple sessions

#### Abu Dhabi Securities Exchange - Trading Hours

| Session Name   | Time Zone   | Start Time   | End Time   |
|----------------|-------------|--------------|------------|
| PRE_MARKET     | Asia/Dubai  | 09:30        | 10:00      |
| MARKET         | Asia/Dubai  | 10:00        | 14:00      |
