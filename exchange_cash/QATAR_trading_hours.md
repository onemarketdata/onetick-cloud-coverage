# Qatar Stock Exchange Trading Hours

The Qatar Stock Exchange (QSE) provides a trading platform for Qatari public shareholding companies’ equities, bonds, and other securities.

Trading Hours are divided into multiple sessions

#### Qatar Stock Exchange - Trading Hours

| Session Name   | Time Zone   | Start Time   | End Time   |
|----------------|-------------|--------------|------------|
| PRE_MARKET     | Asia/Qatar  | 09:00        | 09:30      |
| MARKET         | Asia/Qatar  | 09:30        | 13:16      |
