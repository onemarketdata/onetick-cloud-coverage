# Korea Exchange - Indices Trading Hours

Korea Exchange offers trading in major Korean equity indices like the KOSPI, KOSDAQ and sector/strategy indices.

Trading Hours are divided into multiple sessions

#### Korea Exchange - Indices - Trading Hours

| Session Name   | Time Zone   | Start Time   | End Time   |
|----------------|-------------|--------------|------------|
| PRE_MARKET     | Asia/Seoul  | 07:30        | 09:00      |
| MARKET         | Asia/Seoul  | 09:00        | 15:30      |
| POST_MARKET    | Asia/Seoul  | 15:30        | 18:00      |
