# US Consolidated Equities (exc. OTC) Enumerations

Consolidated data from all US equities exchanges excluding OTC traded securities.  Provides from the US SIP (CTA/UTP)

The following fields have Enumerations:


* AGGRESSOR_SIDE - Indicates whether a trade resulted from an incoming buy or sell order.


* BOOK_TYPE - Type of order book or trading mechanism through which a trade was executed.


* EVENT_TYPE - Type of Event. EARNING_DATE or COMPANY_CONFERENCE_CALL


* QTE_COND / COND (QTE) -


* TRADE_TYPE / COND (TRD) -


* CORR - Type of trade correction / cancellation


* EXCHANGE - Exchange from which the update originates


* FINRA_ADF_IND - Indicates whether the quote reflects the FINRA BBO


* IS_PRE_OPEN - Indicates whether a quote originates from the period before (1) or after (0) the instrument has opened on the primary market


* LULD_BBO_IND - Indicates whether the bid/ask crosses the upper / lower LULD price bands


* LULD_IND - Type of Limit Up / Limit Down (LULD) price update


* NBBO_IND - Indicates whether the quote affects the NBBO


* NBBO_LULD_IND - Indicates whether the NBBO crosses the upper / lower LULD price bands


* REASON_CODE - Reason for an instrument status update


* RESTRICTION_IND - Indicates whether short selling is permitted


* RPI - Indicates the presence of Retail Price Improvement interest on bid and/or ask side


* SECURITY_STATUS_IND - Security status


* SIP_MSG_ID - Identifies messages generated by the SIP (”” - Originates from an exchange, “E” - UTP, “S” - Consolidated Tape System)


* SOURCE - Source from which an update originates


* STOP_STOCK - “Y” - Stopped stock trade, “N” - Not a stopped stock trade, “” - Not applicable


* TRADE_PERIOD - Market period during which a trade was executed.


* TRADING_STATUS - Indicates whether the instrument is subject to a halt or suspension


* TTE - Indicates whether a trade is exempt from the Trade Through Rule (1 - Trade Through Exempt, 0 - No Trade Through Exemption)


* TRF - Trade Reporting Facility used to report a trade (for trades reported to FINRA)
